Backyard Pizza Jam

A Pizza Pop-Up at your house

What is Backyard Pizza Jam?

Backyard Pizza Jam is a pop-up pizza restaurant at your house. Well technically in your backyard. Part dinner party, part cooking class. A participatory, improvisational experience involving lots of melted cheese.

We will transform your backyard into a world-class pizza restuarant before your very eyes.

How does it work?

Before your event we'll help you plan out a menu of pizza types, topping combos and side dishes. The day-of we'll bring everything you need to have an incredible dining experience: 700 degree pizza ovens, slow-fermented dough, fresh toppings and sauces.

Top your own pies, try your hand at tossing dough, or kick back and let us take care of everything. We can show you the ropes or simply man the ovens while you and your guests enjoy fresh out of the oven pizza in the comfort of your own backyard.

About Us

Backyard Pizza Jam started in our own backyards, where we've gradually honed our pizza-making skills over many evenings and many more bottles of wine. The only thing we enjoy more than making pizza is sharing it with friends.

We are excited to share the joy we find slinging 'za with you & yours.
